// Questions

Have Questions?

How does Bullion Algo Systems work? +

Bullion Algo is an automated trading algorithm designed for MT4 and MT5. Once connected via MetaApi, it analyses market conditions and executes trades based on pre-defined logic — removing the need for manual chart analysis or emotional decision-making entirely.

Do I need trading experience to use it? +

No prior trading background is required. The configuration runs autonomously once connected, though monitoring parameters can be modified directly within your integrated account terminal.

Is my capital guaranteed? +

Risk caps are hardcoded into the execution rules, maintaining a strict 1.5% stop allocation per trade structure to protect your initial balance. No system eliminates all risk, but ours is engineered to minimise drawdown.

Can I monitor my account in real time? +

Yes. Your dashboard displays live balance, equity, open positions, trade history, and PnL — all pulled directly from your connected MT5 account via MetaApi. Everything updates in real time.

Which brokers are compatible? +

The setup integrates smoothly with any brokerage running a standard MetaTrader 4 or MetaTrader 5 platform with ultra-low latency configurations enabled. Exness is the recommended broker for optimal copy-trade execution.
